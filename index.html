April 2022

Writing in English!

In most the middle-eastern countries, people do not have a good relationship with the English language, and its obvious reason is native languages of these countries do have not much similarity to English. Iran is no exception to this issue, and furthermore, most of the provinces have their own native languages (e.g. Azerbaijani, Kurdish, Balochi, etc), apart from the national language (Persian or Farsi). So, from most people's point of view, it is really difficult to get used to writing in English; let alone writing exams in English! I used to write my homework and final exams in the English language most of the time, and guess what?! The lecturers always were giving me extra scores because of this!

CROSSFYRE 2021

Sometimes I wonder why I am running too fast. The feeling of being a tiny boy is something that I always have when I am participating in any scientific event, and the reason is obvious; I am the only one who is still a Bachelor's student! You can feel it when you are just a B.Sc. student among Postdoc researchers, faculty members, and maybe Ph.D. candidates.
